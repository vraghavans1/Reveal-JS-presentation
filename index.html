Financial Formula

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ NPV = \sum_{t=0}^{T} \frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t} \]

Compound Interest:

\[ A = P \times \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt} \]

These formulas are critical for evaluating investments and interest growth.
